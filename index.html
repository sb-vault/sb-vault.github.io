How The Vault Works
01
Verify Your Account
Click "Login with Minecraft" — you'll be taken to MC-ID to verify your Minecraft account once. No password needed, just your Minecraft login.
02
Post a Listing
Pick your armour type, select which pieces you're selling, enter each piece's hex colour code. The category is detected automatically.
03
Browse & Offer
Browse active listings and filter by category. Hit "Make Offer" to leave your IGN and offer amount.
04
Trade & Complete
Reach out via Hypixel IGN, agree on a trade, then mark the listing complete. It moves to Completed and builds real price history.
⚠ Safety Notice

The Vault is a community board – we do not hold funds or mediate disputes. Always verify the item before confirming.
