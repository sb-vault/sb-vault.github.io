How The Vault Works
01
Verify Your Account
Click "Login with Minecraft" – you'll be taken to MC-ID to verify your Minecraft account once. No password needed.
02
Post a Listing
Pick your armour type, select pieces, enter hex colour codes. The category is detected automatically from the colour.
03
Browse & Offer
Browse active listings, filter by category. Hit "Offer" to start a chat with the seller and negotiate.
04
Trade & Complete
Reach out via Hypixel IGN, agree on a trade, execute in-game, then mark the listing complete.
⚠ Safety Notice

The Vault is a community board – we do not hold funds or mediate disputes. Always verify the item before confirming.
